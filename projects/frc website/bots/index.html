Innovation In Control

Solis is our on-season robot for CRESCENDO, the 2024 robotics competition. This robot has a fold out intake that feeds into a ajustable note-shooter. There is a defelctor panel to aid in scoreing amp shots and two climbing arms. This robot preformed well at Magnolia and preformed wonderfully at Bayou Reigonal Championship, getting fourth place in qualifications and finalist in playoffs.

Josephine I

Josephine I is our off season robot for CRESCENDO. It has a dual under-bumper intake and a dual directional shooter. It can make speaker shots from very far away and make amp as well. This robot made 11th in qualifications at North-Shore-Knockout and will compete in Red-Stick-Rumble

StrongHold

StrongHold is our robot for the 2016 First Robotics competition. This robot shoot yellow balls and is able to traverse over obsticles in the field.
